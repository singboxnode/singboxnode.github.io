读取文件
import pandas as pd
df_news = pd.read_table(‘E:\机器学习数据集\新闻分类任务\data\val.txt’,names[‘category’,‘theme’,‘URL’,‘content’],encoding=‘utf-8’)

OSError: Initializing from file failed
文件初始化失败

修改：
df_news = pd.read_table(‘E:\机器学习数据集\新闻分类任务\data\val.txt’, names=[‘category’,‘theme’,‘URL’,‘content’],encoding=‘utf-8’,engine = ‘python’)
